Mealmitra For Bridging Hearts and Plates.

Welcome to Mealmitra, your trusted partner in the fight against hunger and food waste. Our mission is to ensure that no one goes to bed hungry, especially children and those who cannot afford a proper meal. As a dedicated platform for food donation, we bring together compassionate individuals and organizations to provide nutritious meals to those in need. Join us in our relentless pursuit to eradicate hunger and bring hope to those in need. With Mealmitra, your generosity can transform lives and create a lasting impact.

How To Help Us

How To Help Us

Join hands with Mealmitra by donating to our cause and helping all living beings. You can also contribute to society by becoming grassroots volunteer at Mealmitra, assisting us in serving those in need. By creating powerful awareness in the minds of people through social media, we can highlight the importance of our organization in serving humanity.

Send Donation

At Mealmitra, we believe giving isn't just about donations; it's about making a meaningful difference. We ensure transparency by providing a detailed account of every rupee you donate. Your support directly impacts lives, empowering us to nourish communities and create lasting change.

Volunteering

Volunteering is the ultimate exercise in democracy. Just as you vote in elections once a year, volunteering allows you to vote every day for the kind of community you want to live in. At Mealmitra, volunteering is a powerful way to actively shape a future where no one goes hungry.

Share Media

Media awareness offers more than just hope; it inspires action. To maximize our impact at Mealmitra, we strive to guide others in translating awareness into tangible support. Together, we can turn compassion into meaningful actions that nourish and uplift communities in need.

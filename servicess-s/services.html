How We End Hunger

Food Access

In 2023, our network distributed billions of meals through initiatives like mobile pantries and Kids Cafes, ensuring people in communities across India get the nourishment they need to thrive.

Food Rescue

We collaborate with food companies and farmers to supply food banks with food that would otherwise go to waste. In 2022 alone, our network rescued 4 billion pounds of food and groceries, ensuring it reached those in need across India.

Disaster Response

When disaster strikes, those facing food insecurity are often the hardest hit. As one of India's largest disaster relief organizations, we provide food and emergency assistance before, during, and after disasters to help communities recover.

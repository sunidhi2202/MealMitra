ABOUT US

Welcome to MealMitra, where our mission is to bridge the gap between food surplus and hunger. At MealMitra, we are dedicated to creating a world where no one goes hungry while perfectly good food goes to waste. Our platform connects generous donors with surplus food to individuals and communities in need, ensuring that every meal counts. We empower individuals and organizations to organize food donation campaigns, make meaningful contributions, and foster a spirit of community and compassion. Through our innovative and user-friendly platform, we aim to make food donation and distribution seamless, transparent, and impactful. Join us in our journey to eliminate hunger and reduce food waste, one meal at a time. Together, we can make a significant difference.

Our Mission

At MealMitra, our mission is to create a sustainable and hunger-free world by leveraging technology and community spirit. We strive to eliminate food waste by facilitating the seamless donation of surplus food from individuals, businesses, and events to those who need it most. Our goal is to ensure that no one goes hungry while good food goes unused.

Our Vision

Our vision at MealMitra is to create a world where everyone has access to nutritious food, and food waste is a thing of the past. We envision a community-driven ecosystem where individuals, businesses, and organizations come together to share surplus food, ensuring that every meal reaches someone in need. By harnessing the power of technology, we aim to build an inclusive platform that not only facilitates food donations but also raises awareness of food waste.

Our Goal

At MealMitra, our goal is to eradicate hunger by efficiently redistributing surplus food to those in need. We aim to create a seamless, user-friendly platform that connects donors with recipients, ensuring that no meal goes to waste. By organizing food donation campaigns and providing real-time information on available food and nearby events, we strive to make nutritious food accessible to all.

Impact Stories

A Successful Food Campaign

University students in Metroville organized a food campaign via MealMitra, redistributing thousands of meals. John, an elderly beneficiary, received consistent, nutritious food, improving his health and lifting his spirits. The campaign highlighted MealMitra’s impact on hunger through community collaboration.

A Community Saved from Hunger

In Greenville, a bakery’s surplus goods reached Maria, a single mother, through MealMitra. The regular donations helped feed her children and save money, enabling her to start a home business. Maria now donates her own baked goods, completing the circle of generosity.
